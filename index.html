creative conference

 from 2015 till 2018 

The main theme of our exhibition is a symbiosis of

innovative  technologies and  creativity

We have formed several teams consisting of directors, choreographs, artists, costume designers, stage designers and technical companies to prepare and demonstrate their act numbers and show elements of the next generation utilising innovative technologies such as multimedia, circus and dance, VR, robots, high-tech stage props.
